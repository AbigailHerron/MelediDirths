About Me

I write worlds that whisper, and stories that linger long after the last page

My work explores new worlds, emotional undercurrents, and the subtle magic that hides in dark corners of fantasy lives.

I’m drawn to stories that unfold slowly — where atmosphere leads, meaning is discovered rather than explained, and silence carries weight.

Based in Ireland, I’m currently developing several projects and documenting the creative process as I go.
