Prosperity's Curse

Status: In Development

Prosperity’s Curse explores a world of deity worship and meddling. When our hero, Salem, fell for the Saintess Lucia, he gets far more than he bargained for.

And because of his reckless actions, his wife, Elenor, must follow him on the path to chaos.

Still in its early stages, this story is forming its voice, themes, and direction — a slow burn with sharp edges.
